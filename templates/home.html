Welcome to Wi-Fi Bastion

Wi-Fi Bastion is a powerful web-based security tool designed to protect users from cyber threats posed by malicious Wi-Fi networks.

The application scans available Wi-Fi networks in real time, detecting and identifying suspicious activities, such as weak encryption, duplicate SSIDs (Evil Twin attacks), and rogue access points.

What Wi-Fi Bastion Detects

Evil Twin Attacks

Detects Wi-Fi networks with duplicate SSIDs that mimic legitimate hotspots to steal user credentials.

Weak/No Encryption

Identifies networks using outdated encryption (WEP) or open Wi-Fi hotspots with no encryption at all.

Rogue Access Points

Detects unauthorized or suspicious access points that could be used for data interception.

How Wi-Fi Bastion Works

Wi-Fi Bastion scans nearby networks and analyzes their security features in real time. The app evaluates:

  • SSID (Network Name) & BSSID (MAC Address)
  • Encryption Type (WPA2, WPA3, WEP, Open)
  • Signal Strength & Anomaly Detection
  • Potential Threats & Warnings
