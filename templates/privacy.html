1. Introduction

Welcome to Wi-Fi Bastion. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you use our application and tell you about your privacy rights.

2. Data We Collect

When you use Wi-Fi Bastion, we collect the following types of information:

  • Wi-Fi Network Information: SSID, BSSID, signal strength, and encryption type of networks detected during scans.
  • Threat Detection Data: Information about potential security threats identified during scans.
  • Blocked Networks: Information about networks you have chosen to block.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our application, including scan frequency and features used.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To detect and prevent potential security threats to your network
  • To improve our application and user experience
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Data Storage and Security

All data collected by Wi-Fi Bastion is stored locally on your device. We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your data.

5. Your Data Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of your personal data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Object to processing of your personal data
  • Request restriction of processing your personal data

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "last updated" date.
